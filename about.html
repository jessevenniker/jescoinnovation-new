Let's talk

One person.
One island.

Not an agency. Not a freelance platform. Just someone who builds websites that work — for businesses that deserve better.

Jesco Innovation is a one-person web design studio based on Curaçao, focused entirely on local entrepreneurs on Curaçao and Bonaire.

Every project is handled directly — design, copy, development, launch. No account managers, no handoffs, no team of six who've never seen your island.

I know
this island.

I grew up watching local businesses struggle with the same problem — they were good at what they did, but their online presence didn't reflect it. Templates that felt generic, agencies that felt distant, prices that felt wrong.

I built Jesco Innovation because I believed local businesses here deserved a different kind of web designer. Someone close. Someone who picks up the phone. Someone whose standard for the work is: would I be proud to show this to someone I know?

That's still the standard. Every project.

— Jesse

The difference

No proposals.
No surprises.
Just the work.

Most agencies make the process feel complicated on purpose. Long intake forms, discovery phases, unclear pricing, and then a website delivered by someone you've never spoken to. You end up paying more for something that's almost what you wanted.

My process is different. We have a conversation. I understand your business. I build something that reflects who you are. You review it before anything goes live. If something isn't right, we fix it.

No contracts with small print. No monthly retainer unless you want one. Direct contact from day one to after launch.

Direct contact

You talk to the person building your site. Not a project manager, not a ticketing system. Just a direct line — phone, WhatsApp, email.

Clear pricing

No vague estimates. You know what you're getting and what it costs before anything starts. No surprises at invoice time.

Still reachable

After your site goes live, I don't disappear. If something needs updating, I'm still available. Your site isn't an archived project — it's an ongoing responsibility.
