Year 2025
Timeline 9 days
Scope Design, copy, development, launch
Website concept — anonymised
Situation

The water was
stunning.

The owner had been running guided dives here for years. Regulars came back every season, sent referrals, wrote emails that read like postcards. But first-time visitors searching online found a website that looked like it hadn't been touched since 2015.

The gap was stark: one of the most beautiful dive sites in the southern Caribbean, represented by a template with stock photos and a booking form that went nowhere.

Approach

Lead with the
feeling.

We built the site around atmosphere first. The water here has a specific colour — teal shifting into deep blue — and the site had to carry that. Full-width imagery, minimal copy, nothing competing with the visual.

Booking was simplified to a single WhatsApp link. No forms, no chatbot, no friction. The copy was rewritten to match how the owner actually talks to guests — direct, warm, personal. No agency language.

Result

A site they're
proud to share.

Within the first two weeks, the owner was sending the site link in replies to enquiry emails — something she'd avoided doing before because the old site "didn't look professional." That small shift said everything.

Fast, clear, and working. Exactly what it needed to be.
